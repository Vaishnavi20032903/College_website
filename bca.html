About Computer Application Department

The BCA course is a full-time three years (six semesters) Bachelor's Degree in Computer Application. The degree helps interested students in setting up a sound academic base for an advanced career in Computer Applications.

The Computer Application course blends computer fundamentals with application development, focusing on software design, databases, web technologies, and mobile app development. Students receive practical exposure to industry-relevant programming languages and frameworks, enabling them to become skilled application developers and IT professionals.

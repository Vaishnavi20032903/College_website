About Computer Science Department

The B.Sc. Computer Science is a three-year undergraduate program that provides comprehensive knowledge in computer science fundamentals, including algorithms, data structures, programming languages, and system design.

This program emphasizes theoretical foundations alongside practical applications, preparing students for careers in software development, system analysis, research, and advanced computing technologies. Students gain expertise in problem-solving, logical thinking, and cutting-edge computing concepts.

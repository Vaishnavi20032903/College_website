About M.Sc. Computer Science Department

The Master of Computer Science (M.Sc. CS) is a two-year postgraduate program focused on advanced theoretical and practical aspects of computer science, emphasizing research methodology and specialized knowledge areas.

This program is designed for students who wish to pursue research careers or advanced technical positions, covering areas such as advanced algorithms, computational theory, machine learning, distributed systems, and emerging computing paradigms.

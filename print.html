About Printing Technology Department

The Printing Technology program combines traditional printing methods with cutting-edge digital technologies, preparing students for careers in the evolving printing and media industry.

Students learn about digital printing, offset printing, packaging design, color management, prepress operations, and modern printing workflows, gaining both technical expertise and creative design skills essential for the printing industry.

Our Inspiration

Behind every great institution are visionary leaders who inspire, guide, and shape its journey towards excellence. These distinguished educators have been the pillars of strength for CASAS Department.

Guided by Visionary Leadership

Leadership is not about being in charge, but about taking care of those in your charge. Our principals have exemplified this philosophy through their unwavering dedication to academic excellence, student welfare, and institutional growth.

Their wisdom, experience, and commitment have been instrumental in shaping CASAS Department into a center of learning that nurtures not just academic achievement, but character, innovation, and social responsibility.

Dr. B. B. Sagade

Current Principal

Dr. Balasaheb Babanrao Sagade serves as our esteemed Principal, bringing a wealth of knowledge and experience in English literature and academia. With his M.Phil. and Ph.D. in English, he has established himself as a distinguished scholar in American Literature, Ecocriticism, and Tribal Literature.

Key Achievements & Contributions:

  • Erasmus Mundus Scholar - visited Spain (2011)
  • Active Senate Member of Savitribai Phule Pune University
  • Coordinator of EFSLE for Maharashtra region
  • Extensive research presentations at national and international conferences
  • Dedicated advocate for quality education and student development

Under his leadership, CASAS Department has flourished as a center of academic excellence, fostering an environment where students are encouraged to explore, innovate, and achieve their full potential. His vision for holistic education continues to inspire both faculty and students alike.

Dr. B. H. Zaware

Former Principal

Dr. B. H. Zaware, our revered former Principal, laid the foundation of excellence that continues to define our institution today. With over 37 years in higher education and 8 years as Principal, his transformative leadership elevated the college to unprecedented heights.

Legacy of Excellence:

  • Achieved A++ Grade with CGPA 3.79 by NAAC (2017)
  • Secured substantial funding (Rs. 308 lakhs) from various government schemes
  • Established 13 new academic programs during tenure
  • Recognized Research Guide at Savitribai Phule Pune University
  • Published 23 research articles and 8 books
  • NAAC Peer Team Member (nominated 2018)

His commitment to community service, women empowerment, and academic innovation created a lasting impact. Dr. Zaware's philosophy of education for all, especially the underprivileged, continues to guide our mission of inclusive and transformative education.

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world. Our role as educators is to nurture minds, inspire hearts, and build character."

— Philosophy that guides our leadership
